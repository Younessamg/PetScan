Learn more about resources and what we do

PetScan Only is a pet health diagnostic tool designed to help and assist pet owners in an immediate fashion. It provides quick testing for common conditions in dogs, including conjunctivitis, ear infections, bloating, and kennel cough. PetScan Only aims to empower pet owners with valuable information about their pet's health.

For more detailed information on pet health, users are encouraged to refer to reputable sources such as:

  • Veterinary Partner
  • CAPC (Companion Animal Parasite Council)
  • AVMA (American Veterinary Medical Association)
  • WebMD Pet Health Community
  • AAHA (American Animal Hospital Association)
